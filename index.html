Step-by-Step Guide to Boiling Eggs

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect boiled egg every time:

  1. Start with Room Temperature Eggs: Take your eggs out of the fridge 20-30 minutes before boiling to prevent cracking.
  2. Boil Water: Bring a pot of water to a rolling boil. Add a pinch of salt to help with peeling.
  3. Gently Add Eggs: Use a spoon to lower the eggs into the boiling water to avoid cracking.
  4. Set the Timer: Use the Eggquisite Timer to set the exact time for your desired doneness (3, 6, 8, or 10 minutes).
  5. Prepare an Ice Bath: While the eggs cook, fill a bowl with ice water to stop the cooking process immediately after boiling.
  6. Cool the Eggs: Once the timer goes off, transfer the eggs to the ice bath for 5 minutes.
  7. Peel and Enjoy: Gently tap the eggs on a hard surface to crack the shell, then peel under running water for easier removal.

  • Flavor Boost: Add a splash of vinegar or soy sauce to the boiling water for a subtle flavor infusion.
